# Source Paper Notes

This repo bootstraps from the ideas summarized in the original 2024 methods paper and related repo notes:

- fragmentation spectra are used as 550-dimensional representations
- responses are brain- and lung-homing log2 fold changes
- PLSR is used to learn interpretable latent structure
- KEGG/Fiehn projections help interpret the learned space

The 2.0 paper should build on that foundation rather than merely restating it.
